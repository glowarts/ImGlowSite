Worldbuilding

Hobby work

Initially starting out as a rock sculpting exercise. This pack evolved into being a full-blown kit for making stylized pine forests.
I took a lot of inspiration from Overwatch's artstyle with this. Tried mimmicking their pbr values as much as I could whilst still trying to make it my own. I wanted to create a pack that you could easily use to make natural looking pine forests. And for that I needed a lot of different kinds of foliage. This pack contains different kinds of trees, bushes, weeds, plants, rocks and mushrooms. As well as a set of textures built into Unreal Engines Terrain Material tool.

Worldbuilding with Figma

To get a better overview, I decided to try to visualize the magic forces into a cosmology chart. To achieve this I used a popular interface design tool called Figma.
