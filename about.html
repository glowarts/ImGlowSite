SUMMARY


Greetings! I am Marcus Ian Damgaard Rasmussen, a 3D artist currently studying at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts & Architecture in Copenhagen.

My interests lie in video games, stylized art, and the fantastical realm of imagination. I am fascinated by environment art and worldbuilding, and can spend countless hours tweaking the smallest of details to create an immersive and captivating experience. Nevertheless, I understand the importance of focusing on the bigger picture and know when it's time to move on.
As an artist, I am always in search of new software to help bring my ideas to life.

My creative journey began making maps for games like Starcraft 1 & 2, Warcraft 3, and Half Life 1 & 2 without any formal design training. However, my passion for 3D environment art and design was soon discovered, and I haven't looked back since.

(except for that time I took up dubstep production for a little bit but we dont talk about that)


When not studying, I spend most of my time listening to music, working on creative projects, and experimenting with new tools to improve my craft. I frequently explore in-game worlds, both to admire the scenery and seek inspiration for my own art.

Thank you for taking the time to peruse my work! If you enjoy what you see, please consider following me to stay abreast of my latest projects.
