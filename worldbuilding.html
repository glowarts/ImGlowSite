Worldbuilding

Hobby work

One of my biggest interests lies with Worldbuilding. I can spend a lot of time tinkering with the lore of fictive universes.
One big project of mine has been in the works over several years. It's a fantasy universe with an intricate magic system.
One of my big ambitions is to one day be able to turn it into a game. And therefore i'm putting a lot of effort into making sense of the lore in the universe
This is because I want consistency in the final design, as well as in the lore.

Worldbuilding with Figma

To get a better overview, I decided to try to visualize the magic forces into a cosmology chart. To achieve this I used a popular interface design tool called Figma.
